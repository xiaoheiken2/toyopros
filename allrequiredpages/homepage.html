How We Remove Mold


Mold remediation is a complex task that requires careful planning, execution, and attention to detail. At EcoMold Removal Services in #State, we follow a step-by-step approach:

  • Assessment and Isolation: Our certified professionals will conduct a thorough inspection to determine the mold's extent and find the moisture sources. We then isolate the affected areas using plastic sheeting and negative air pressure.
  • Preparation and Containment: Our team suits up in proper PPE and seals off contaminated areas. Negative air pressure systems help trap airborne spores.
  • Mold Removal and Cleaning: Using professional tools, we remove all visible mold from surfaces and materials. We then sanitize the area to eliminate any lingering spores.
  • Air Filtration and Drying: We deploy HEPA air scrubbers to capture spores and use drying equipment to remove remaining moisture—crucial for preventing mold regrowth.
  • Restoration and Repair: Damaged drywall, insulation, or other materials may be replaced as part of the restoration process.
  • Final Inspection and Clearance: We perform a final check to ensure the mold is gone, and provide a detailed report with maintenance tips.
  • Our project managers will keep you updated every step of the way. We prioritize speed and quality to ensure the process causes minimal disruption.
